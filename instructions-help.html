Troubleshooting WiFi

Begin at your WiFi Router to make sure that a hard reset isn't the easy solution:

  1. 1. After reaching your router, remove the power cord for 30 to 45 seconds
  2. 2. Replace the power cord and give the router 3 to 5 minutes to properly reboot and resynchronize.
  3. 3. Check to see that the online button is lighted in a solid color:
  4. 4. If the online button is on, check your connection and if you are not connected, proceed to the next section.

Return to your Computer and follow the following troubleshooting instructions:

  1. 1. Right click the WiFi mini icon on your taskbar and click "troubleshooting problems":
  2. 2. Proceed to click "troubleshoot my connection to the internet":
  3. 3. The troubleshooter will reset your IP address and revalidate your connection. The following result should appear if this fixed the problem:
  4. 4. If the troubleshooter reads “not fixed” then you likely have a larger problem and should navigate to our contact page and report your issue.
